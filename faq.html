Questions, answered

Anything else? Message us through the store — we read everything.

How do I receive my files?

Instantly. After checkout you'll see a download page, and Payhip also emails you a download link that never expires. No physical item ships.

What sizes are included?

Every product lists its sizes on the product page — planners come in US Letter, A4 and A5 at minimum (P1 adds Happy Planner size), and wall art comes in five print ratios covering frames from A5 up to 24×36.

How do I print at 'actual size'?

In your print dialog choose 100% / Actual size — not 'fit to page'. Pick the file matching your paper (Letter or A4) and it will print true.

Can I use these in GoodNotes or Notability?

Yes — beautifully. Our planners have clickable contents pages and sidebar bookmarks, so they navigate like an app. Import the PDF and tap away.

Are the colors printer-friendly?

Yes. Backgrounds stay white, lines stay thin, and color appears only in accents — the ink-friendly choice is designed in, not an afterthought. Kids products additionally include a pure black & white version.

What's your license — can I share the files?

Personal use: print as many copies as you like for yourself and your household (teachers: your own classroom is fine). Please don't resell, redistribute or share the files themselves.

Do you offer refunds?

Because files are delivered instantly, all sales are final — but if anything is wrong with your files, message us and we'll make it right. We read everything.

Is the Qur'an content authentic?

Surah names, ayah counts and juz data are machine-verified against the Tanzil Qur'an database (Kufan/Hafs numbering, 6,236 ayat), and we deliberately avoid printing ayah text on pages meant to be used up and discarded.

How does checkout work?

Checkout is handled securely by Payhip (cards via Stripe/PayPal). You never need an account — just an email address for your download link.

Ramadan 2027 — when should I start preparing?

Ramadan 2027 begins around February 7. Our Ramadan planner and kids' activity book launch November 1, 2026 — early enough to plan the season calmly.
